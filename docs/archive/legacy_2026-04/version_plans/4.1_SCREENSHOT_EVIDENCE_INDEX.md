### `4_1_home_entry.png`

- Home page entry surface.
- Confirms:

  - the "continue questionnaire" CTA is visible;
  - the home card explicitly states that questionnaire linkage is additive only.

### `4_1_questionnaire_page_q1.png`

- Questionnaire entry page, first question.
- Confirms:
  - the module is the non-official four-dimensional questionnaire;

  - the non-official disclaimer is present;
  - the page renders a real question and answer options.

- Questionnaire progression page, second question.

- Immediate post-submit state on the questionnaire surface.
- Confirms:

### `4_1_result_route.png`

### `4_1_history_page.png`
