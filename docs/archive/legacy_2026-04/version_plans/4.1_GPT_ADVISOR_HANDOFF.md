## What the advisor should know

### Product shape

- 4.1 is a non-official questionnaire capability, not an official MBTI product.
- The module is intentionally lightweight and explainable.
- The homepage and match linkage are additive surfaces only.

### Protected surfaces

The following surfaces must remain intact in any follow-up work:

- `POST /api/v1/profile/basic`
- `GET /api/v1/profile/astro/summary`
- `GET /api/v1/profile/astro/chart`
- `GET /api/v1/app/health`
- `GET /api/v1/app/version/check`
- 4.0 media / storage / attachment pipeline
- 3.x archived truth-chain boundaries

### Intentional non-goals

- official MBTI claim or official MBTI productization;
- large personality-system expansion;
- dynamic feed;
- full image/video message product;
- audio/video calling;
- major match-score redesign.
