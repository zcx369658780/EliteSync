# EliteSync 5.2 Scope And Non Goals

## Scope

5.2 implements the smallest useful Me/Profile expression upgrade:

- Tag expression system grouped into product-readable sections
- Personal operating hub for profile, status, and relationship readiness
- AI display advice upgraded from a single entry to actionable suggestions
- AI draft helper for self-introduction, greeting, and highlight organization
- Light voice expression candidate slot
- Lightweight personal appearance layer preview
- 5.2 documentation and evidence index scaffolding

## Explicit Non Goals

- No Laravel backend change
- No database schema, migration, seed, restore, or write operation
- No profile canonical truth rewrite
- No Match, Chat, Notification, Message, Media, RTC, or LiveKit contract change
- No automatic AI sending, auto dating assistant, or server queue
- No voice platform, video, live streaming, or multiplayer RTC expansion
- No marketplace, coin system, paid dressing, or commercial center
- No repo-level rollback
- No release/version chain update unless a later release task explicitly requires it

## Protected Surfaces

5.2 may enhance Me/Profile but must keep the modern UI baseline and existing navigation intact. Settings remains the place for privacy, account, and security. Profile appearance is only a lightweight expression layer.

## 5.1 Observation Handling

5.1 observations are not the main development scope of 5.2. They may be tracked as later evidence or small verification items, but 5.2 should not rebuild 5.1 relationship returnflow, notification returnflow, or RTC rhythm features.
